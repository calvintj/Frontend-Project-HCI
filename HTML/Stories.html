Cloud

A brave and hardworking young man. His priority is to protect the village from Voldemort. He does not has much potential like Gerald, but his persistancy and the will to never give up is second to no one.

Gerald

Cloud's best friend, born with gifted talent. He always compete with Cloud to be the best and protect Cloud from danger. His father is killed by Voldemort, getting revenge is his priority.

Voldemort

An evil sorcerer who seek for greatness in this world. He killed innocent people to find more power, include Gerald's father. His ultimate goal is become a supreme sorcerer.
